    Instruction:

  • Click "Start Game" to start game, click "Restart" to restart game.
  • Press WASD or Arrows to move.
  • Press P to pause/resume game.
  • Press E to change weapon.
  • Little Green Circle is Health Pack, pick to recover player's health.
  • Little Blue Circle is Ammo Pack, pick to recover player's ammo.
  • Dark Grey Block is Stone, unbreakable.
  • Brown Block is a wall, breakable.
  • Large Light Grey Square is the roof of a house, will become transparent when player enters.
  • Large Green Square is Grass, will halve the player's speed.
  • Large Blue Square is Ice, will double the player's speed.
  • Enemy:
    • Grey Enemy doesn't move or attack.
    • Pink Enemy uses a Pistol and will continue chasing the player.
    • Orange Enemy uses a Shotgun and will chase the player when they are in its sight range, won't be affected by Grass or Ice.
    • Blue Enemy uses a Sniper Rifle and will chase the player and attack with it's hands when it has no ammo, won't be affected by Grass and Ice.
